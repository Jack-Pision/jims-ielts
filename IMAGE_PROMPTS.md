# JIMS IELTS Website Image Prompts

All images should have a slightly cool-to-neutral tone, professional and aspirational aesthetic. Colors should complement the emerald green palette naturally.

---

## 01 · FOUNDER PORTRAIT · ABOUT PAGE HERO

**Usage:** About page hero section, right side split
**Aspect Ratio:** 3:4 (Portrait) — 900×1200px minimum, 1200×1600px recommended

**Prompt:
> Professional portrait of a confident Bangladeshi educator, mid-40s, wearing a dark navy or charcoal suit with a crisp white shirt. Shot against a deep emerald green (#041A0C) seamless background. Soft key light from the upper left, slight rim light from the right creating subtle highlights on the shoulder. Shallow depth of field with background softly blurred. Expression: calm, authoritative yet approachable, with a gentle confident smile. The subject faces slightly to the left, looking directly at camera. This is a man students trust with their futures. High-end corporate portrait style.

**Color Notes:** Deep emerald background (#041A0C), warm skin tones, professional lighting

## 02 · IELTS EXAM SHEET · HERO SECTION BACKGROUND

**Usage:** Optional background texture for hero section
**Aspect Ratio:** 4:3 (Landscape) — 1600×1200px or 1920×1440px

**Prompt:
> Close-up macro photograph of an official IELTS answer sheet on a dark walnut wooden desk — a sharpened graphite pencil resting diagonally on top, answers partially filled in with careful handwriting. Shot at a low 20-degree angle from the side. Shallow DOF, text blurred at edges with only the pencil and nearest answers in sharp focus. Warm directional desk lamp light from the left creating soft shadows. Dark, moody, serious atmosphere. Represents preparation, precision, and focus. Colors: warm browns, off-white paper, dark graphite grays.

**Color Notes:** Dark wood tones, warm lighting, paper off-white - complements emerald theme

## 03 · CLASSROOM SESSION · HOME / ABOUT

**Usage:** Why JIMS section, or Institute Story section
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (Widescreen) — 1600×900px or 1920×1080px

**Prompt:
> Wide shot of a small, focused IELTS classroom — 6–8 students at individual desks arranged in rows, all facing a whiteboard at the front. Shot from the back of the room, slightly elevated angle. Natural window light from the left side creating soft illumination. The instructor stands at the whiteboard, mid-gesture explaining a concept. No faces fully visible — privacy maintained, backs of heads or side profiles only. The space feels engaged, purposeful, and professional. Clean modern classroom with deep green accent wall matching #063020. Minimalist desks, organized atmosphere.

**Color Notes:** Emerald green accent wall (#063020), neutral beige/cream desks, natural lighting

## NOTES FOR IMAGE GENERATION

1. **Avoid** overly saturated, bright colors that clash with the emerald theme
2. **Prefer** slightly desaturated, professional tones
3. **Maintain** warm-to-neutral color temperatures
4. **Ensure** all people depicted are respectfully represented
5. **Focus** on authentic moments rather than stock-photo poses
6. **Backgrounds** should be clean and not distracting from the main subject
